-Welcome to my website!
I am a high school student from a suburb of Chicago who will be living in Germany for a cultural exchange during the 2015- 2016 calendar year! How you might ask? I am a recipient of the CBYX- YFU scholarship. I am honored to be selected among the thousands of amazing applicants. All of which were brave enough to push themselves to new limits.

As I go through this amazing journey I will be documenting my experiences on my blog. I have a passion for computer science and have programmed this site "from scratch". As I grow throughout this year I hope my knowledge of computer science will also grow with me. If you have any questions and would like to contact me, I can be reached by my email.
